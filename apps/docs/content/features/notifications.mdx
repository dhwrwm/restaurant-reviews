# Notifications

<Callout type="warning">**Status:** 📋 Planned — no delivery mechanism, model, or UI exists today.</Callout>

## Overview

Would notify restaurant owners of new reviews, and potentially reviewers of
replies or moderation outcomes.

## Purpose

Right now an owner only learns about a new review by checking their
dashboard. Notifications close that loop, but the delivery mechanism
(email vs. in-app vs. push) and volume are still open questions, and the
feature is speculative until those are settled.

## Planned shape

- Sending a notification shouldn't block the request that triggered it
  (e.g. review creation) — this is the concrete use case that motivates a
  background job queue. See
  [Phase 3 — Redis & Go Services](/roadmap/phase-3-redis-go-services) and
  [Target Architecture: Background Jobs](/architecture/target#background-jobs).
- Likely starts as email-only (lowest infrastructure cost), with in-app
  notifications as a later addition once there's a job queue to build on.

## Trade-offs

Not yet applicable — nothing is built.
