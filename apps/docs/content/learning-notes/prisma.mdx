# Learning Notes: Prisma

- Declaring a second `generator` block pointed at
  `packages/types/src/generated/prisma` turned out to be a clean way to
  share just the enums with the frontend, without either duplicating them
  by hand or bundling the full Prisma runtime into `apps/web`. Full writeup:
  [Database: Prisma Schema](/database/prisma-schema).
- `prisma migrate deploy` being a safe no-op when there's nothing pending is
  what makes running it at the front of Render's `startCommand` (instead of
  a dedicated pre-deploy step, unavailable on the free tier) actually safe —
  see [Database: Migrations](/database/migrations).
